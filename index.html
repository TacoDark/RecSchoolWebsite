What is Recschool?

Recschool is more than just a game. It is a creative playground where you set the rules. Just some fun for everyone!

Inspired by the legacy of Rec Room (RIP), Recschool was created to carry on the legacy of Rec Room, We miss the classic days just as much as you do, which is why we built this from the ground up to bring that magic back for a new generation!
